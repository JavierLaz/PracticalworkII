Fundamentals of Computer Engineering

The subject "Fundamentals of Computer Engineering" is created to provide a basic understunding of Computer Engineering, both as an academic subject and as a future profession. It aims to teach students the foundational principles of this field, including mathematical, physical, anthropological, and ethical aspects. Moreover, it shows career opportunities and the role of computer engineers in modern society.

Theoretical Foundations

The fundamentals of computer engineering subject offers an extensive education on the different aspects of technology and that includes the theoretical functioning and developments of each field.

Practical skills

This subject, through the practical works, group activities and individual tasks, does also teach different skills curcial for a computer engineering such as

  • Teamwork and collaboration
  • Ethical awareness
  • Communication skills

Practical works

1.Investigation group work

A set of tasks assigned by the teacher with the objective of nurturing teamwork and collaboration among classmates.
The Practical Work I consisted of 3 sections:

  • The technical document
    A text document where all the information gathered by the team about the chosen topic was presented in an orderly manner .
    The document had to address the history of the technology, the technologies employed, the advantages and disadvantages and finally, an ethical overview of the topic.
  • The oral presentation:
    An exposition of the topic which aimed to give a clear explanation of the topic and each of it's aspects. The presentation also consisted of specific questions about the topic that allowed the group to show their mastery over the topic.
